Easy Installation

Installation RTM
Download the RTM on your PC, open the program and select your API (TMAPI, CCAPI, HEN) add your console IP then connect & attach, enjoy !
Installation SPRX
Download the SPRX on your PC, put the file "MC.sprx" to dev_hdd0/tmp and the "EBOOT.BIN" on your Minecraft folder (for ex: dev_hdd0/game/NPEB01899/URSDIR).
